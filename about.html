Our Story

IS601 Services Limited is a contemporary services company providing services such as construction, perimeter barriers and securities and facilities management. We have evolved in the scope and delivery of our services to have in our clientele high net worth individuals, reputable multinational corporations and government agencies.

  • IS601 is a fast growing company, with project presence in states of the federation.
  • IS601 was incorporated and registered by the Corporate Affairs Commission in the year 2017.
  • IS601 Services Limited is a contemporary services company.

Business Philosophy.

We believe in building more than structures—we build trust, quality, and lasting relationships. With a focus on innovation, sustainability, and excellence, our mission is to create spaces that inspire and endure.

Speed of Response, Accuracy and Clarity.

Instant response- That’s what you need. That’s what you get. We can supply you with layouts, proposals in any format such as on disk, on paper or direct to you by e-mail. Besides the speed of response, clear, accurate and positive information – whether it is the specifications of the items that we recommend and select to performance expectations is also essential. That’s what you will receive.

Range – Types and Variants.

We know that this is critical to your business to have just the right item that is functional, yet merges perfectly with the décor that you have worked so hard to create. No more eyesores that detract from the impact of your designs. You want variety, compatibility, performance; and that is what you will get.

Input on Every Project on a Turnkey Basis.

All your projects are important. We supply the proposal detailing selection, location, positioning, specifications, prices, performance and parameters. Literally, we offer a one-stop turnkey security solution.

State of the art, Upgradeable yet the most Cost Effective Solutions.

We understand that it enhances your reputation to incorporate only the best technically and financially. Hence we search and develop international tie-ups with the best in the field that are compatible with local physical conditions, yet are cost effective.
