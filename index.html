Brief History.

IS601 Services Limited is a civil contracting company, with expertise in preventive security systems; civil/structural construction; facility management. The Company works with her clients to design, build, operate and maintain energy-efficient and environmentally-friendly facilities.

The Company offers services in various sectors, such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, industrial, energy and public sectors.
